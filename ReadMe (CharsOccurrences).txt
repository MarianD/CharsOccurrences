CharsOccurrences v 1.0.2 - plugin for Total Commander 5.51 and newer.
---------------------------------------------------------------------
This plugin is freeware.


Description:
------------
Shows the occurrences of letters in the file, ordered both
alphabetically and by occurrences, both horizontally and
vertically.

In vertical display shows also percentages of occurrences
and the sum of all occurrences.


Limitations:
------------
In the current version of the plugin it shows only occurrences
of the English ASCII letters, case insensitively.

No settings are meanwhile provided.


Usage:
------
In Total Commander press F3 or Ctrl+Q. Maybe you will have
then choose "Options | Image/Multimedia", perhaps repeatedly,
if another plugins have higher priority.
